Why Us?

MyNutriAdvisor is a free, online service providing personalized health and fitness recommendations. We are a group of developers trying to make the world a healthier place using code. While people have known for some time now that technology can promote health – consider wearable fitness trackers and smartphone pedometers – we have decided to take the idea a step further. Using a sophisticated algorithm and a short, unobtrusive questionnaire, we can offer you practical suggestions designed to target your individual needs. This application is designed for the individual who seeks to improve overall health, across four general categories: nutrition, exercise, habits, and sleep. With only five minutes of your time, we can help you to live a healthier life.
